Our History

Founded in 2009, Innovative Process Technologies has steadily grown into a reliable and recognized name in the field of industrial valves, pumps, and process equipment. Under the leadership of Mr Manoj Gole, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering quality products and dependable service across India.

Mr. Gole brings over 24 years of extensive experience in both domestic and international markets, including significant professional exposure in the Middle East, particularly in Dubai. His deep understanding of pumps, valves, sealants, and industrial systems has played a key role in shaping the company’s growth.

The company specializes in technical sales, industrial solutions, and business development, offering services such as coolant filtration systems, component cleaning machines, and customized engineering solutions tailored to client requirements.

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction, Innovative Process Technologies has developed a wide network of clients, agents, and partners across the country. The company is known for its ability to handle complex requirements, provide timely delivery, and maintain long-term professional relationships.

Through consistent dedication, technical expertise, and a customer-first approach, the company continues to expand its presence in the industrial sector while maintaining high standards of quality and performance.
